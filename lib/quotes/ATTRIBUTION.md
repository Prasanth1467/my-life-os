# Quote sources (bundled offline)

Curated quotes live in `lib/quotes/curated.json`:

- **Krishna / Gita**: widely published English renderings of the Bhagavad Gita (public-domain style translations; chapter references are approximate labels for personal use).
- **Luffy / anime**: short attributed lines from fiction for personal motivation; verify wording against your preferred subtitles if you need exact dialogue.
- **Discipline / long-term**: mix of proverbs and commonly cited modern habit authors where noted.

Review and edit the JSON to match your taste and any attribution requirements.
